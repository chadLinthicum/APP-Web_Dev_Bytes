Refund Policy

Refund Policy for Web Dev Bytes

At Web Dev Bytes, we aim to ensure your complete satisfaction with our services. If you're not satisfied, please review our refund policy below.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be issued under the following conditions:

  • The request for a refund is made within 30 days of the purchase date.
  • The services provided did not meet the agreed-upon specifications.
  • There is a billing error or unauthorized transaction.

Process for Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our support team at wdbytes@gmail.com.
  • Provide your order number and details of the issue.
  • Wait for a confirmation email from us regarding your refund request.

Refund Approval Timeline

Once your request is received, we will process it within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase.

Exceptions to the Refund Policy

Refunds are not applicable in the following cases:

  • If the service has been partially or fully completed as per the agreement.
  • If the request exceeds 30 days from the date of purchase.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding our refund policy, please contact us:

Email: wdbytes@gmail.com
