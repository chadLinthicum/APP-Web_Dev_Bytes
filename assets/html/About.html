About

At Web Dev Bytes, our mission is to empower local businesses through innovative tailored digital solutions that enhance online presence and drive growth, helping each business reach their full potential in the digital landscape!

Meet the Team

Chad and Emily Linthicum are a dynamic duo rooted in the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountain region of Virginia. Growing up surrounded by the stunning landscapes of their hometown, they developed a deep appreciation for their community and its vibrant spirit. This connection drives their passion for giving back and supporting local businesses.

Emily is a talented photographer and brand marketing connoisseur, bringing creativity and vision to every project. Her eye for detail and knack for storytelling help businesses showcase their unique identities. Meanwhile, Chad focuses on the technical side, ensuring that clients have strong, functional online presences that truly reflect their brands.

Together, they are committed to helping businesses thrive. They believe that by combining their skills, they can foster growth and innovation within their community. Their mission is to empower local enterprises to reach their fullest potential, creating a brighter future for everyone in the Blue Ridge region.
